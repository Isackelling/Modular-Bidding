## B. Scope of Responsibility — Why This Warranty Is Divided

Because your home was built in a manufacturing facility, two separate parties are responsible for different portions of the construction:

**The Manufacturer** constructed the home unit in a factory, including:
- Structural framing, roof, and exterior shell
- Factory-installed interior finishes, cabinetry, flooring
- Plumbing rough-in and electrical rough-in completed at the factory
- Factory-installed appliances and mechanical systems

**Sherman Builders** is responsible for:
- Foundation design coordination and construction
- Delivery coordination and transportation permitting
- Crane and set operations (placing the home on the foundation)
- Marriage wall assembly (multi-section homes)
- Final utility connections (electric, plumbing, HVAC, fuel)
- All finish work completed on-site
- Site cleanup and final inspection coordination

Defects in **manufacturer-supplied components or factory workmanship are the responsibility of the Manufacturer**, not Sherman Builders. Sherman Builders will assist the Owner in pursuing manufacturer warranty claims but does not assume liability for factory defects.

## D. Applicable Building Standards — HUD Code vs. MN State Building Code

The applicable construction standards depend on the type of home:

**HUD-Code Manufactured Home:**
> Factory-built portions of a HUD-code manufactured home are constructed under **federal HUD Manufactured Home Construction and Safety Standards (24 CFR Part 3280)**. Federal HUD standards may govern warranty rights for factory-built components in ways that differ from or supersede Minnesota Chapter 327A. An attorney should be consulted to confirm which warranty rules govern the factory-built portions of a HUD-code home.

**Modular Home:**
> A modular home is factory-built to **Minnesota State Building Code** standards. Minnesota Chapter 327A applies in full to both site work and factory-built portions. The Manufacturer and Sherman Builders each warrant their respective scopes of work under Chapter 327A.

**Site Work (both home types):**
> All site work performed by Sherman Builders complies with the **Minnesota State Building Code** and applicable local codes, regardless of home type.

The HUD Data Plate and/or certification label(s) must be present on the completed unit at delivery. If any label is missing, notify Sherman Builders immediately.

## E. Warranty Date — Factory-Built Home Clarification

**For Sherman Builders' site work warranties**, the Warranty Date is the earlier of:
- (a) The date the Owner first occupies the home; or
- (b) The date the Owner takes legal or equitable title to the home.

**Important:** For a factory-built home, the Owner may take title to the home unit (as personal property or a titled asset) **before site installation is complete**. To prevent the warranty clock from starting prematurely, the Warranty Date for Sherman Builders' site work warranties shall not begin earlier than the **date the home unit is set on the foundation and site work commences.**

**For the Manufacturer's warranty**, the warranty date and trigger are governed by the Manufacturer's warranty document (Exhibit W-1). The Manufacturer's warranty date may differ from Sherman Builders' Warranty Date.

If there is any question about which warranty date applies to a specific defect, contact Sherman Builders and the Manufacturer in writing.

## F. Claims Procedures

### F.1 — Claims Against Sherman Builders (Site Work & Installation)

1. **Report in writing** within six months of discovering the defect. Send written notice to:
   > Sherman Builders | 2244 Hwy 65, Mora, MN 55051
2. Sherman Builders will **inspect within 30 days** of receiving written notice.
3. Sherman Builders will provide a **written offer to repair within 15 days** of completing the inspection.
4. If the parties agree on scope of repair, Sherman Builders will perform repairs per the written agreement.
5. If the parties do not agree, the matter proceeds to the **MN Home Warranty Dispute Resolution Process** under MN Stat. § 327A.051 before any court action may be filed.

### F.2 — Claims Against the Manufacturer (Factory-Built Components)

1. Contact the Manufacturer directly using the contact information in Section C.
2. Notify Sherman Builders in writing at the same time.
3. Follow the Manufacturer's claims procedure as set forth in Exhibit W-1.
4. Sherman Builders will assist in coordinating access, documentation, and follow-up.

### F.3 — Disputed Claims (Origin Unclear)

If it is not clear whether a defect originated in factory construction or site installation, notify **both** Sherman Builders and the Manufacturer in writing. Both parties will cooperate to inspect and determine the responsible party. The Owner's claim rights are preserved against both parties during this process.

## G. Warranty Exclusions

The liability of Sherman Builders under this warranty is limited to site work and installation defects and does not extend to the following:

- (a) Loss or damage **not reported in writing within six months** after discovery;
- (b) Loss or damage caused by **defects in design, materials, or installation supplied or directed by the Owner**;
- (c) **Secondary loss or damage** such as personal injury or property damage;
- (d) Loss or damage from **normal wear and tear**;
- (e) Loss or damage from **normal shrinkage** within building standard tolerances;
- (f) Loss or damage from **dampness or condensation** due to insufficient ventilation after occupancy;
- (g) Loss or damage from **negligence, improper maintenance, or alteration** by parties other than Sherman Builders;
- (h) Loss or damage from **changes in site grading** by parties other than Sherman Builders;
- (i) **Landscaping or insect** loss or damage;
- (j) Loss or damage from **failure to maintain** the home in good repair;
- (k) Loss or damage where the Owner has not taken **timely action to minimize** damage;
- (l) Loss or damage after the home is **no longer used primarily as a residence**;
- (m) **Acts of God**, including fire, explosion, smoke, water escape, windstorm, hail, lightning, falling trees, aircraft, vehicles, flood, and earthquake — except where caused by failure to comply with building standards;
- (n) Loss or damage from soil movement **compensated by legislation or covered by insurance**;
- (o) Loss or damage due to **soil conditions on Owner-supplied land** obtained independently of Sherman Builders;
- **(p) Defects in manufacturer-supplied components, factory workmanship, or materials originating in the manufacturing facility** — these are the responsibility of the Manufacturer and are governed by the Manufacturer's warranty (Exhibit W-1).
